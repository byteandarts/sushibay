## Notes

- **Format:** All images in WebP for optimal web performance
- **Color Palette — Café Supreme:** Warm tones (amber, brown, gold, #6B3A2A, #C8956C)
- **Color Palette — Sushi Bay:** Cool tones (teal, deep blue, slate, #1c424a, #B3904E)
- **Style:** Dark, moody, cinematic food photography — consistent with the luxury dining aesthetic
- **Resolution:** Generate at 2x (2400×1600) and compress to WebP at quality 80 for retina displays
- **Menu item images** (referenced in cafe.json and sushi.json) should be placed in `images/menu/` directory. Each should be a clean food photo at 800×500px on a dark background matching the restaurant's aesthetic.
